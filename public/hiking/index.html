Hiking in Kenya

Explore the breathtaking landscapes of Kenya through hiking

Mount Kenya

Mount Kenya, Africa's second-highest peak, offers a variety of trails for hikers. It's known for its stunning glacial features, diverse flora, and unique wildlife.

Aberdare Ranges

The Aberdare Ranges are a lush and scenic mountain range. Hike through dense forests, waterfalls, and enjoy encounters with elephants, buffalos, and diverse birdlife.

Mount Longonot

Mount Longonot is a dormant volcano with a unique crater. The hike to the summit rewards you with panoramic views of the Great Rift Valley and Lake Naivasha.
